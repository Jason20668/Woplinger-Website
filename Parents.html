Our Commitment

We’re committed to building a strong, skilled, and supportive lacrosse program. Our coaches, players, and families work together to grow both on and off the field. The work you put in the off season really makes a difference so take advantage of these games!

  • Summer Centercourt Freeplay games
  • Summer Goodsports League to compete games
  • Fall Harvest League games
  • Weekly winter Training and Centercourt League games
