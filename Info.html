Information

TeamSnap

We use TeamSnap to manage games and practices, track attendance, and keep everyone updated with team events and activities. Make sure your player keeps their TeamSnap attendance up to date for all games and practices.

Hudl

Hudl is our team's video analysis platform. Coaches upload game film so players can review footage, study plays, and improve their performance. Players can also use Hudl to build highlight reels for college recruiting purposes.

Google Classroom

Google Classroom is where coaches share important team documents, updates, and resources. Make sure you are enrolled and checking in regularly so you don't miss any announcements or materials posted by the coaching staff.

Fundraisers & Events

Our program depends on the support and involvement of families. Please be ready to step up and help at events throughout the season, including:

  • Pasta Nights
  • Buffalo Wild Wings Team Dinner
  • Gatorade and water refills in team locker room
